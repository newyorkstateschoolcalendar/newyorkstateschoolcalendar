Spring Awakening: March â€“ May

Spring in Bucks County is a magical time, as the landscape awakens from its winter slumber. Expect a flurry of events celebrating the season’s rebirth:

  • Maple Sugaring Season (March-April): Several farms throughout the county open their doors to visitors, offering tours of their sugar houses and opportunities to sample delicious maple syrup and other treats made from this sweet nectar. Check local farm websites for specific dates and times. Many offer pancake breakfasts and family-friendly activities.

  • Easter Egg Hunts (Late March/Early April): Numerous towns and organizations host Easter egg hunts for children of all ages. These events often include other spring-themed activities, such as face painting and crafts. Look for announcements from local parks, churches, and community centers closer to the date.

  • Bucks County Wine Trail Spring Events (April-May): The numerous wineries dotted across the county typically host spring events, including wine tastings, live music performances, and food pairings. Check the Bucks County Wine Trail website for a detailed schedule of events as they are announced. Many wineries also offer beautiful vineyard tours and scenic views.

  • Opening of Outdoor Spaces and Gardens (April-May): As the weather warms, Bucks County’s many beautiful gardens and parks open their gates to visitors. Consult their individual websites for specific opening dates and any special events they might be hosting.

  • Spring Art Shows and Festivals (April-May): Numerous galleries and art centers throughout the county host spring art shows and exhibitions, showcasing local and regional artists. Keep an eye on local arts publications and websites for announcements. The New Hope Arts Center is a reliable source of information for upcoming art events.

Summer Celebrations: June â€“ August

Summer in Bucks County is a whirlwind of outdoor activities, festivals, and performances:

  • New Hope Celebrates (June): This annual celebration typically features a parade, live music, street performers, and various vendors. It’s a vibrant display of community spirit and a great opportunity to experience the charm of New Hope.

  • Farmers Markets (June-October): Numerous farmers markets operate throughout the summer, offering fresh, locally grown produce, baked goods, and crafts. Check your local newspaper or online listings for the nearest market and its operating schedule.

  • Delaware River Boat Tours (June-August): Enjoy scenic boat tours on the Delaware River, offering stunning views of the Bucks County landscape. Several companies offer various tours, from leisurely cruises to more adventurous excursions.
